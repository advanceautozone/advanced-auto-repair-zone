Brake Service & Repair

Trust Advanced Auto Repair Zone to inspect, repair, and replace your brakes so you can drive with confidence.

Schedule Brake Inspection

Signs you may need brake service

  • Squealing or grinding noises when you stop
  • Brake pedal feels soft, spongy, or goes to the floor
  • Vibration or pulsation when braking
  • Vehicle pulls to one side when stopping

What we inspect

Our technicians perform a complete brake inspection, including:

  • Brake pads and shoes
  • Rotors and drums
  • Calipers, wheel cylinders and hardware
  • Brake fluid condition and leaks
  • Brake hoses and lines
